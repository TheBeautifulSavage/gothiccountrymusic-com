Where old country roads meet eternal darkness. Gothic country — raw, haunting, and unapologetically American. Murder ballads, Southern Gothic tales, and the beauty that lives in shadow.

What Is Gothic Country?

Gothic country is the dark soul of American roots music — a genre that fuses traditional country instrumentation with the brooding atmosphere of gothic literature, Southern Gothic storytelling, and folk's most haunting traditions. It dwells in the shadows: murder ballads, tales of sin and redemption, lonely roads, and the beauty found in darkness.
